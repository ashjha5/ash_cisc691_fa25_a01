The Hound of the Baskervilles is perhaps the most famous of the Sherlock Holmes adventures.
Sherlock Holmes investigates a supernatural legend on the eerie moors of Devonshire.
Watson accompanies him, documenting every detail of the investigation with his characteristic thoroughness.
The atmosphere is thick with fog, mystery, and an air of impending doom.
Holmes uses his remarkable powers of deduction to unravel the truth behind the ghostly hound.
Sir Henry Baskerville's life hangs in the balance as the detective pursues the villain.
