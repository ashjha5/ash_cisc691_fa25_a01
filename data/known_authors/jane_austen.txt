Pride and Prejudice remains one of the most beloved classics of English literature.
Elizabeth Bennet is a spirited and intelligent protagonist who refuses to compromise her principles.
The novel explores themes of love, class, social expectations, and personal growth.
Mr. Darcy initially appears proud and disagreeable to Elizabeth's family and friends.
Through a series of misunderstandings and revelations, their relationship develops beautifully.
Austen's wit and social commentary shine through in every chapter of this timeless romance.
